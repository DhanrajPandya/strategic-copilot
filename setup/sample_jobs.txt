Each posting is labelled STRONG FIT or NEAR MISS with the reason, so the
profile builder can learn where Dhan's real boundary sits. Strong fits should

illustrative examples of the kinds of roles Dhan is (and is not) targeting.

JOB 1 - STRONG FIT (enterprise, in-house, at-level, core function)
Title: Head of Digital Workplace
Company: Global Specialty Insurer (FTSE 250, London)
=====================================================================
Reporting to the CIO, you will own the strategy, roadmap and delivery of the
end-to-end digital workplace for 8,000 employees across the UK, Europe and Asia.
You will lead a function of ~35 people spanning End User Computing, Service Desk
and Collaboration, with full P&L and a multi-million-pound budget.

Responsibilities:
- Define and deliver the Digital Employee Experience (DEX) strategy, using
  Nexthink to measure and continuously improve experience-level agreements (XLAs).
- Drive the Modern Workplace roadmap: Microsoft 365, Windows 11, Intune,
  Autopilot and Azure AD / Entra ID.
- Mature ITSM on ServiceNow; embed shift-left, self-service and automation.
- Champion the "voice of the user"; own executive-level experience reporting.
- Lead supplier management, contract negotiation and vendor rationalisation.
- Build and develop a high-performing leadership team.

Requirements: 10+ years' senior EUC / digital workplace leadership; DEX/Nexthink
experience; strong M365/Intune background; ITIL; regulated-sector experience
preferred. Location: London, hybrid.
